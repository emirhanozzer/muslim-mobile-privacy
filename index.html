Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Samuf Studios ("we", "our", or "us") built the Muslim Mobile app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

Muslim Mobile requests access to your device's location to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your area
  • Determine the Qibla direction

Your location data is processed locally on your device and is not sent to our servers. We do not store or share your precise location with any third party.

Locally Stored Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Prayer tracking records
  • Makeup prayer (Qada) records
  • Tasbih counter data
  • Your preferences (language, notification settings)

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Usage data

For more information on how Google handles your data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Geocoding Service

The app uses a geocoding service to convert your coordinates into a city name for display purposes. Only approximate location data is used for this purpose.

Permissions We Use

  • Location – To calculate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • Internet – To display ads and retrieve city name from coordinates
  • Notifications – To send prayer time reminders (with your permission)
  • Compass/Motion Sensors – To determine Qibla direction
  • Audio Playback – To play Quran recitations

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as described in the Third-Party Services section above (Google AdMob).

Data Security

All your personal data (prayer records, preferences) is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that store user data. The security of this data depends on your device's own security measures.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is suitable for all ages, and no account creation or personal data submission is required to use it.

Your Choices

  • You can revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. Prayer times will use your last known location.
  • You can clear all app data by uninstalling the application.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
